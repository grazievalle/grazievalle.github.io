My Journey into Artificial Intelligence

June 3, 2026 · 3 min read

Artificial Intelligence has transformed from a niche research field into one of the most impactful technologies of our time. This blog marks the beginning of my journey documenting what I learn while building AI systems, machine learning models, and software projects.

Why AI?

My interest in AI started with a simple question: how can machines learn from experience? As I explored machine learning, deep learning, and large language models, I realized AI sits at the intersection of mathematics, software engineering, and creativity.

What I'm Learning

  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Deep Learning with PyTorch
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • AI Agents and RAG Systems
  • MLOps and Deployment

Current Projects

I'm currently working on projects involving reinforcement learning, computer vision, and AI agents. Building projects has been the fastest way for me to understand concepts beyond theory.

What's Next?

Future posts will cover machine learning concepts, deep learning architectures, AI agent frameworks, MLOps practices, and lessons learned from real-world projects.

Thanks for reading. This is the first post in what I hope becomes a long journey of learning and building.
